The Cloud CMS for Jekyll

CloudCannon has everything you need to build, host and update Jekyll websites

Get started free

Fits your workflow

Synchronise CloudCannon with your Jekyll site on GitHub, Bitbucket or Dropbox. When developers commit changes, CloudCannon pulls the repository and rebuilds your site. Changes editors make on CloudCannon are committed back to your repository.

Easy editing for your team

Editors update content directly on the page with no guessing how changes appear on the live site. Developers control what clients update by adding a class of editable to HTML elements and using front matter.

The tools you love

Build sites using Jekyll, Liquid, Sass and any CSS or JavaScript frameworks you like. Develop in your local environment then sync to CloudCannon with a git push.

Live, scalable and secure

CloudCannon builds your Jekyll site, minifies your assets and publishes it live on our supercharged hosting.
